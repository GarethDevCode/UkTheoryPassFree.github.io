PURPOSE

I decided to set up this site in early 2022 after I passed my thoery test. When studying for it, I was surprised to discover many websites lock their content away behind a paywall. Considering that all UK drivers are mandated by law to know these facts, it is quite startling that some websites try to withhold the information. I decided to compile the required information into a single easy to use resource to help other learners pass their tests. It already costs far too much to learn how to drive. Save your money for real world driving lessons, don't pay for online courses.
