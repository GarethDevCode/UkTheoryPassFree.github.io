PURPOSE

This site is intended as a free and easy to navigate resource for the UK Driving Theory test. ... All UK drivers are mandated by law to know these essential facts. As such, it should be easy for every UK citizen to access. Yes, there are many other websites that do this: however, many of which require registration and/or payment. This adds an unnecesary step to an already difficult and costly process. It is worth mentioning that the UK goverment offers free study material for leaner drivers. While this is helpful, navigation can be obtuse and practice content is unavailable for free - except some minor samples. For more information please see the subsequent section.
